wordcloud

Small calculator to approximate the share of rent each tenant should pay, given the area of their room and the total rent of the appartment. It is also possible to add extra shared taxes like intenet or council tax to the calculation for budgeting purposes. The app is meant to be a first guess at what a fare share of rent would be. It doesn't take into account features like insuite bathrooms, balcony, etc..

Task tracker

Python app to track time spent on tasks. One can add what taks it wished to track, and the amount to time spent on each task each day. It is meant to be a tracking app for desktop only, to avoid interactig with a phone. It also allows to plot time spent on each task, to be able to visualize progress and consistance.
